Here is an Example of the Recipe Format to follow: 
each detail is important (including the colons, spaces,
and newlines, etc..),  failure to adhere strictly to 
this format will result in a failure to import the recipe.
-----------------------------------------------------
Name: Fried Chicken
Meal Type: Dinner
Core Item: Chicken
 
Ingredients:
Chicken breasts 3 pounds
Flour 4 cups
Seasoning 3 tsp
    
Steps:
1) Cut Chicken into Strips
2) Coat chicken with flour
3) Fry until crispy
